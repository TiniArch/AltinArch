Administrative complex with living green roof, biophilic design principles, and near-zero energy performance through triple-glazed facade systems.

01 — Concept

Design Vision

Nature reintegrated into institutional architecture — a low-lying building whose living roof merges with the surrounding park, drawing daylight deep into open-plan floors while achieving near-zero carbon through passive systems and triple-glazed facades.
